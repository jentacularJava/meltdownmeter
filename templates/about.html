About Meltdown Meter

Meltdown Meter is a tool to track behaviour. It can help with identifying and managing behaviours through regular observation. When you aim to change a child's behaviour, the first step is to identify the patterns in behaviour and to measure a baseline so you can track how their behaviour changes as you change your own behaviour.

Click "Add Entry" and answer the questions. Only the date/time and child's behaviour fields are required. I highly recommend you complete the other fields as well, but if you only have time to complete the one field, it's better than nothing.

This can be used to track any behaviour. Meltdown Meter sounded catchy so it stuck.
